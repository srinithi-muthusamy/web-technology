Welcome to LeaveEase

LeaveApp is your one-stop solution for managing employee leaves efficiently. With our platform, you can:

  • Apply for leave easily.
  • Track the status of your leave applications.
  • Manage leave types and departments.
  • Administer employee information effectively.

Managers can efficiently track leave systems using LeaveApp:

  • Review and approve leave requests.
  • Monitor leave balances and histories.
  • Generate reports for leave analytics.

HR management capabilities include:

  • Managing leave types and policies.
  • Updating department information.
  • Managing employee records.
